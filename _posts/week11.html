- Enhancement (figure): What the medium amplifies or intensifies. For example, radio amplifies news and music via sound. - Obsolescence (ground): What the medium drives out of prominence. Radio reduces the prominence of print and the visual. - Retrieval (figure): What the medium recovers which was previously lost. Radio returns the spoken word to the forefront. - Reversal (ground): What the medium does when pushed to its limits.
