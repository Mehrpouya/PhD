VW Owners

Come along for a cup of persian tea and saffron biscuits and talk about the recent VW incident

We are carrying out a study to understand how or whether your attitudes towards VW have changed following the recent news on VW faking carbon emissions through the software installed in them.

If you own a VW car* that has been affected by this event and you are based in Edinburgh area I would like to talk to you about how or whether your attitudes towards VW have changed.

Car Models:

  • 2-Liter four-cylinder diesel
  • 2009-2014 Jettas, 2010-2014 Golfs, 2012 to 2014 Beetles known as Generation 1
  • Passats from the 2012 to 2014 model years known as Generation 2.
  • 2015 Jetta, Golf, Beetle and Passat cars known as Generation 3

When:If you are available between 28th of October 2015 - 6th November 2015

Location: To be confirmed based on your preference.

I am a PhD student at University of Edinburgh. This is part of a larger research and I want to learn about your views and feelings about the recent VW incident.

Your name and data will be anonymised. The interview will take between half an hour to forty minutes.

Please send me an email about your prefered time and location to be interviewed and I will get in touch asap for confirmation.
